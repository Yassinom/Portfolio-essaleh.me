Light Mode Colors
    Background: #f5f5f5 (very light gray) or #ffffff (white)
    Primary Text: #333333 (dark gray for better readability than pure black)
    Secondary Text: #666666 (medium gray)
    Accent: #007acc (muted blue) or #3498db (calm blue)
    Highlight or Button Background: #e0e0e0 (soft gray)



Dark Mode Colors
    Background: #121212 (almost black) or #1e1e1e (dark gray)
    Primary Text: #e0e0e0 (light gray for readability)
    Secondary Text: #a0a0a0 (medium gray)
    Accent: #66d9ef (soft cyan) or #bb86fc (muted purple)
    Highlight or Button Background: #333333 (dark gray, slightly lighter than background)
